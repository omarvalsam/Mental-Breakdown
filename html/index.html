Welcome to MentalBreakdown Project Manager!

Our job is to take the stress out of your projects by providing a way to break them down step by step. When you utilize MentalBreakdown, rest assured that you can handle even the heaviest of workloads if you approach it one piece at a time. We'll break it down, so you don't.

Breakdown Tasks

Take a big project and break it down into more palatable pieces to prevent overwhelm.

Assign to Team Members

Each task can be assigned to individual team members or kept within a group scope to maintain organization and accountability.

Stay on Track

Track the status of each task by changing it's status throughout the development process from "To Do" to "Done".
